---
title: AI will take our jobs??
---

AI is evolving fast — and yeah, it _can_ replace repetitive tasks.  
But at the same time, it’s also creating new opportunities for people who know how to use it.

Think of it like this:  
AI won’t take your job… someone using AI will.

## Example

Here’s some dummy code:

```ts
function greet(name: string) {
  return `Hello, ${name}! 👋`;
}

console.log(greet("World"));
```
